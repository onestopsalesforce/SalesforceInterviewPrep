Apex Governor Limits in Salesforce

This blog discusses the governor limits in Salesforce, including both synchronous and asynchronous limits. It's important to understand these limits to write efficient and scalable code in Salesforce.

Key Apex Governor Limits

Salesforce imposes certain **governor limits** to ensure that no single customer monopolizes shared resources and that the system remains reliable and efficient. Below are the key governor limits you should be aware of:

Reference: Apex Developer Guide
